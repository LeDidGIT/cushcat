Why CushCat?

CushCat started as a simple and joyful project between a father and his daughter. The idea was born from a plush toy that meant something special in our daily life.

What began as a fun experiment to learn how blockchain tokens work slowly became a project we genuinely enjoy building together. CushCat is not driven by hype, but by curiosity, creativity and the desire to build something transparent and positive step by step.

The goal is simple: create a small community project built with honesty, patience and long-term vision.
